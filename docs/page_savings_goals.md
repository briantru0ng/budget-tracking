# Savings Goals

Track progress toward savings targets with optional compound interest projections for HYSA/investment accounts.

## Top Metrics

When goals exist: total goals count, total target, total saved, and overall progress percentage.

## Active Goals Tab

Each goal is an expandable card showing:

- Progress bar and percentage
- Target amount, current amount, remaining
- Deadline and days remaining
- Interest rate and interest earned (if applicable)
- Status: Completed, Overdue, Urgent (< 30 days), On Track, or Behind Schedule
- Donut chart of saved vs. remaining

Actions per goal:
- **Edit** — change target amount or date
- **Add Contribution** — record a deposit (triggers milestone celebrations with balloons)
- **Delete** — remove the goal entirely
- **See Projection** — shows how much interest will contribute vs. manual deposits

Monthly needed is calculated accounting for interest if a rate is set.

## Add New Goal Tab

Fields: name, target amount, target date, category (emergency, vacation, house, car, education, wedding, general), priority (high/medium/low), starting amount, and interest rate (% APY).

## Contribute Tab

Quick contribution form — pick a goal, enter an amount, date, and optional notes. Milestones (25%, 50%, 75%, 100%) trigger celebrations.

## Allocation Suggestion Tab

Enter a monthly savings budget and get a smart allocation across all active goals based on priority and urgency. Shows a grouped bar chart comparing suggested vs. needed contributions, plus surplus/deficit messaging.
