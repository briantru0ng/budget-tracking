# Loan Tracker

Manage debt with payoff timelines, strategy comparisons, and payment recording.

## Top Metrics

Total debt, total monthly payment, and total interest paid across all loans.

## Active Loans Tab

Each loan is an expandable card showing:

- Current balance, original principal, interest rate, monthly payment
- Payoff date and months remaining
- Total interest over the life of the loan
- Progress bar (percentage paid off)

**"What if I pay extra?"** — enter an extra monthly amount to see how many months and how much interest you'd save.

**Record Payment** — log a payment with date, amount, and optional extra principal.

## Add Loan Tab

Fields: name, original amount, interest rate (%), monthly payment, start date, and loan type (personal, student, auto, mortgage, credit card).

## Strategies Tab

Two debt payoff strategies:

- **Avalanche** — highest interest rate first (mathematically optimal, saves the most money)
- **Snowball** — smallest balance first (psychological wins, builds momentum)

Each loan is ranked with its priority number, balance, rate, minimum payment, and a recommendation.

## Compare Payoff Tab

Select a loan and run a comparison to see how extra monthly payments ($50, $100, $200, etc.) affect:

- Months to payoff
- Total interest paid
- Interest saved vs. minimum payments

Includes a bar chart of interest saved and a summary of the best option.

### Auto-Detect Loan Payments

Scan your transaction history for past loan payments by keyword. Matches are automatically recorded in the payment history. Useful for backfilling payment data after importing bank statements.

## Payment History Tab

Chronological table of all recorded payments across all loans: date, amount, principal portion, interest portion, and balance after payment.
